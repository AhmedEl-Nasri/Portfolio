What I do?

As an undergraduate Mechatronics Engineer at Mansoura University, I specialize in robotics, programming, and embedded systems. Over the years, I have honed my skills in a variety of fields, blending technical expertise with creativity to solve problems and innovate. My journey includes mastering diverse programming languages, excelling in robotics projects, and sharing knowledge through teaching and mentorship.

Robotics & Embedded Systems

I am highly skilled in robotics and embedded systems, with hands-on experience in Arduino technology and designing intelligent systems. From participating in tournament,to mentoring students in hackathons, I bring innovative solutions to complex challenges.

Programming & Automation

I have self-taught and mastered eight programming languages, including C , C++, JavaScript, JQuery, Python, HTML, CSS and Pascal with a strong focus on automation and control systems. My contributions to Mansoura Robotics include working in the C Programming Department, Arduino development, and ensuring quality control for programming and robotics scripts.

Creative Media & Script Writing

I combine technical expertise with creativity, excelling in Photoshop, video editing, and script writing. These skills allow me to create compelling visuals and stories, whether for educational purposes, project presentations, or promotional materials.

Teaching & Mentorship

I am passionate about sharing knowledge, having served as an instructor in robotics and an assistant in programming during hackathons. I thrive in mentoring others, helping students and peers understand complex concepts in robotics and programming while fostering innovation and teamwork.
